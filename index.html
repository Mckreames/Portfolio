About Me

Hi, my name is Will! I'm a passionate and dedicated web developer with a strong commitment to create outstanding web experiences. With over a year of self-taught experience in the industry, I've had the privilege of working on small business websites, personal apps, and even a web game. My journey in IT began with my dad who helped to show me the endless possibilities in the world of tech

My first crack at web development started during my highschool years when I tinkered with HTML and CSS to build my first personal website using Dream Weaver. I was instantly drawn to the art of turning lines of code into visually appealing and functional websites. After graduating, I went on to major in Networking and Computer Science in college. It was there that I came back into contact with coding and realized how much I loved the challenge.

My skill set spans a wide range of technologies and tools. I'm proficient in front-end technologies like HTML5, CSS3, SASS, JavaScript, TypeScript, Bootstrap, Redux, and React.JS. On the back-end, I have experience with Node.JS and have limited experience using MySQL. I also have experience in the use of the version control system, Git.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with different groups of clients and industries. My portfolio showcases some of my most notable projects, highlighting my ability to create functional web applications. Each project tells a unique story of collaboration, problem-solving, and innovation.

I'm always eager to take on new challenges and collaborate on exciting projects. Whether it's creating a new website or enhancing an existing one, I'm willing to take the plunge. Let's start a conversation about how I can help bring your digital vision to life. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to Developing Your Future!
