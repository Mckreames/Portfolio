About Me

Hi, my name is Will! I'm a passionate and dedicated web developer with a strong commitment to create outstanding web experiences. Pass The Pig

Pass The Pig

Pass The Pig is a fun, easy to play game that requires two players. Each player has a stored score that is safe and a current score that is volatile. When it is the player's turn, they will roll the dice until they feel comfortable. Every roll is added to the current score. After the player is done, they "Hold". This adds their current score to the stored score and gives the dice to the other player. If the player gets too greedy and rolls too many times, they increase their chances of rolling a 1. If a player rolls a 1, their volatile score will fall to 0, no points will be added to their stored score, and the other player gets the dice. The first player to reach 100 points wins! This project incorporates HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
